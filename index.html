About us

Polish Cafe has been the top Fast food retail since its very beginning, five years ago and has continued to lead in healthy foods and creative promotions around Europe.
In addition to satisfying your hunger and quenching your thirst, we thrive to make our environment fun-full and stress free. That's why we offer you a price cut on all chicken burgers on Wednesday and Friday nights.
